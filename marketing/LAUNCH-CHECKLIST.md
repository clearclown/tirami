## Messaging Rules

1. **Lead with "free earning"** — "Your PC earns compute credits for free"
2. **Never say "token"** — say "Compute Unit" or "CU"
3. **Always compare to Bitcoin** — "same economics, useful work"
4. **Show real output** — include the demo curl commands
5. **Link to pip install** — lowest friction entry point
6. **Mention safety** — kill switch, circuit breakers (trust signal)
